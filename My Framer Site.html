My Buss

20 Years of Empowering Life

From Vision to Legacy

In 2006, Buss was founded on a simple belief: every Malaysian deserves the chance to be financially protected, secure, and free.

What began with helping families understand the importance of life insurance has grown into guiding a new generation, building real financial security and legacies that last.

Today, that same belief powers Buss as the largest ALC platform in Southeast Asia, united by our mission: Protect & Build.

Buss Through The Years

2005

A Bold Beginning

Partnered with Allianz Life Malaysia, becoming the first to market their investment-linked products.

2006

Gaining Momentum

Achieved RM6 million ANP with 111 associates in our first full year.

2007

Expanding Reach

Opened new branches in Seremban, Penang & Melaka. Buss begins its nationwide footprint.

2008

Empowering High Earners

Launched the Million Dollar Income Team (MDiT), redefining growth and success.

2009

Building Strength

Grew to 609 associates, united by a shared mission to help more families.

2010

Deepening Presence

New branches in Johor Bahru and Bukit Mertajam, strengthening our southern network.

2012

Driving Consistency

Introduced the Consistent Producer Club (CPC) and expanded to East Malaysia, opening in Kota Kinabalu and Kuching.
